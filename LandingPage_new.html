Study smarter, remember longer.

HazelNet is a web-based flashcard system built for structured learning. Create decks, study spaced repetition, and track progress across devices.

FEATURES

Discover our App

Create Decks

Organize your learning by creating custom decks tailored to your subjects, courses, or topics. HazelNet lets you structure your study materials in a way that fits your learning style.

  • Organize your flashcards by topic
  • Customize deck settings
  • Manage multiple decks effortlessly

Create Cards

Build flashcards quickly and easily to capture key concepts, definitions, and questions. HazelNet makes card creation simple so you can focus more on learning and less on setup.

  • Add and edit flashcards effortlessly
  • Designed for effective spaced repetition learning
  • Use clear question-and-answer formats

Check your Stats

Track your learning progress with detailed statistics and insights. HazelNet helps you understand your strengths, identify difficult topics, and improve your study habits over time.

  • View study progress and recall accuracy
  • Monitor review history and study patterns
  • Identify cards that need more practice
