HazelNet was built to help students learn effectively without limitations.

We're glad you're here!

HazelNet is to provide a free, intuitive, and adaptive flashcard application that helps learners study efficiently while retaining information longer. We aim to remove financial barriers, reduce usability complexity, and deliver a learning experience grounded in scientific research.

HazelNet is developed by a collaborative team of Computer Science students, each contributing their expertise to create a reliable and user-friendly learning system.

About Us

Our platform is built around spaced repetition, a proven learning method in cognitive psychology that improves long-term memory. By implementing the Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (FSRS) algorithm and enhancing it with machine learning techniques, HazelNet intelligently schedules flashcard reviews based on each user's performance and learning pace.
